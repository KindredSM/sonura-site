House Music Maker FAQ

what is house music?

house music is an electronic dance genre characterized by a four-on-the-floor beat pattern, typically at 120-130 bpm. originated in chicago in the 1980s, house features repetitive 4/4 beats, groovy basslines, and synthesized melodies. subgenres include tech house, deep house, progressive house, and more.

what bpm should house music be?

house music typically ranges from 120-130 bpm, with 128 bpm being the most common tempo. tech house often sits at 125-128 bpm, while deep house can be slightly slower at 120-125 bpm.
