Techno Music Maker FAQ

what is techno music?

techno is a genre of electronic dance music characterized by repetitive beats, synthesized sounds, and a focus on rhythm and atmosphere. typically 120-150 bpm, techno emphasizes the hypnotic groove with minimal melodic elements. subgenres include detroit techno, industrial techno, minimal techno, and acid techno.

what bpm should techno be?

techno typically ranges from 120-150 bpm. minimal techno sits around 125-130 bpm, while harder industrial techno can reach 140-150 bpm. sonura can generate techno at any tempo you specify.
