Trap Beat Maker FAQ

What is a trap beat?

Trap beats are characterized by rolling hi-hats, heavy 808 bass, crisp snares, and dark atmospheric melodies. Originated in Atlanta hip-hop, trap has become one of the most popular genres in modern music. Typical tempo is 130-160 bpm with half-time feel.

What BPM should trap beats be?

Trap beats typically range from 130-160 BPM, with 140 BPM being the most common. Sonura understands BPM specifications - just include it in your prompt like "trap beat 140 bpm" and the AI will match it.
