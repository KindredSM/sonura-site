Drill Beat Maker FAQ

what is drill music?

drill is a subgenre of hip hop that originated in chicago and evolved in the uk. uk drill typically features 138-145 bpm with sliding 808s, dark piano melodies, and syncopated hi-hat patterns. chicago drill is slightly slower at 60-70 bpm (double-time 120-140) with aggressive lyrics and hard-hitting production.

what bpm should drill beats be?
